# **App Name**: HCOM Connect

## Core Features:

- Mission Display: Showcase HCOM's mission, vision, and core values prominently on the landing page.
- Departments Overview: List comprehensive descriptions of all departments within the company
- AI-Powered Assistant: Implement a generative AI-powered virtual assistant to answer frequently asked questions about HCOM's services and initiatives. The tool should understand the context of the question and provide accurate, helpful responses, as well as guide users to relevant resources on the site.
- Platform Information: Detailed information about the HCOM HealthTech App and the PAHC (Personalized AI-Driven Health Companion), with clear calls to action to download/learn more.
- Outreach Highlights: Highlight the Health Community Outreach Movement, showcasing its impact through stories, photos, and upcoming events.
- RAFFIM Program Details: Present information on the RAFFIM health investment program, outlining its benefits and how to participate.
- Contact Form: Include a secure contact form for inquiries from potential partners, investors, or clients.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) to evoke a sense of health, trust, and technological advancement.
- Background color: Light teal (#E0F8F8), almost white, for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Pale cyan (#77DDE7) to highlight key interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', sans-serif, for a contemporary and fashionable look.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, which has a modern and slightly warm and approachable feel
- Use clean, modern icons to represent various services and departments.
- Ensure a responsive layout that adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes.
- Incorporate subtle animations for a modern feel.
